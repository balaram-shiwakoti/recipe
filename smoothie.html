Fruit and Yogurt Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup yogurt
  • ¼ cup pineapple juice
  • 1 ½ teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon milk

Steps

  1. Add straberry and mash banana
  2. Add yoghurt to the recipe
  3. Finally serve with pineapple
